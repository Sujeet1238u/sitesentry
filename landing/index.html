How It Works

Get started in 3 simple steps.

1. Install SiteSentry

Load the extension in Chrome and pin it to your toolbar.

2. Browse as Usual

SiteSentry quietly tracks how much time you spend on each site.

3. View Insights & Goals

Open the popup to see your stats and how close you are to your focus goal.

FAQ

Is SiteSentry really free?

Yes, it’s completely free to use.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome storage.

How do I install the extension?

Open chrome://extensions → Turn on Developer mode → Click “Load unpacked” → Select the extension folder.
